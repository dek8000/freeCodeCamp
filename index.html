Living Mission Statement

The beauty of being in direct sales and leadership for 10 years is I've honed my ability to share and explain ideas in a simple way. Perhaps one of the most important distinctions I've made is the more passion and engagement you have with a particular product or service, the more effective you are in presenting it. There are certainly many different strategies to use when presenting to maximize how effective you are, given your audience, but no amount of effective strategies can replace genuine enthusiasm, because humans are emotional creatures. I love helping others understand new ideas, the more complex the better! There are so many different segments of the technology industry that I find joy in learning and discussing. I can just so clearly see the exponential degree of growth throughout nearly every facet of the field. For example, I can truly say I have never been more passionate about an idea than the transformative concept that Bitcoin and other blockchains/cryptocurrencies will fundamentally change the way our planet transfers value between humans, both individuals and institutions alike.
